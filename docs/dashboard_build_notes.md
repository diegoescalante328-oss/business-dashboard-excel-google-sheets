# Dashboard Build Notes (Excel / Google Sheets)

## CSV files to import
- `data/dashboard_ready_data.csv`
- `dashboard/kpi_summary.csv`
- `dashboard/monthly_summary.csv`
- `dashboard/category_summary.csv`

## Suggested pivot tables
1. Revenue and estimated profit by month.
2. Revenue, expenses, and profit by product category.
3. Revenue by region.
4. Revenue by sales channel.

## Suggested charts
- Line chart: monthly revenue vs estimated profit.
- Clustered bar chart: revenue by category.
- Horizontal bar chart: revenue by region.
- Donut chart: revenue share by sales channel.

## Suggested KPI cards
- Total Revenue
- Total Expenses
- Estimated Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Units Sold
- Average Order Value

## Recommended dashboard layout
- Top row: KPI cards.
- Middle row: monthly trend chart.
- Bottom-left: category performance chart/table.
- Bottom-right: region/channel chart/table.

## Useful filters/slicers
- Month
- Region
- Product category
- Sales channel
- Customer segment
